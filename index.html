MVP -yeah you know me-


I'm a London-based all-round digital analyst and developer. Born and raised in Flanders, Belgium, I moved to the UK in 2010 and set up my own currency exchange business Leftover Currency. I'm a pro-EU campaigner. In 2016, I snapped up a list of Vote Leave domain names, including VoteLeave.com, and directed them to Rick Astley's Never Gonna Give You Up.

My next project: Cabin Coins

In 2017 I plan to launch a new project, Cabin Coins. It will complement my currency exchange business Leftover Currency. The Cabin Coins site will allow travelers to buy coins at discount prices, below their actual exchange value.

I'm taking a Front End Web Development course at General Assembly in London. I hope this course will allow me to build a good part of the new Cabin Coins site myself.
