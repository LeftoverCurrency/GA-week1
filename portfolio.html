Past and upcoming projects

Here is an overview of past projects I completed, and upcoming projects I am currently working on. In 2017 I am following a Front End Web Development course at General Assembly in London.

Rick Astley

In 2016 I supported the British artist Rick Astley by directing hundreds of thousand of Vote Leave supporters to a video of him singing Never Gonna Give You Up

Leftover Currency

In 2016 I launched the redesigned version of my currency exchange business Leftover Currency, which I founded in 2012. Leftover Currency converts foreign coins, old banknotes and obsolete currencies to cash, quickly and easily.

Relaxr

In 2016 I decided to take stock images and Lorem ipsum text to its limits. The green water in the swimming pool at the Brazil Olympics gave me the inspiration for this exciting project. After two long pizza nights, Relaxr was born.

Startup Matchmaker

In 2015, Inspired by the For Dummies books, I wanted to do something with the colour palet of yellow, white, black and grey. Startup Matchmaker was a perfect match and provided me with a beautiful brick background stock picture.

CitiPix

CitiPix allows the user to enter a city name and click update. Although nothing really happens when a user clicks the update button, it was a great project to finally use that skyline stock image.
